Clinical Shift Scratchpad

A small mobile prototype for keeping active ward jobs out of scraps of paper, messages to yourself, screenshots and memory.

View source

What it is

A scratchpad for active jobs: quick free-text entry, optional minimum-necessary patient identifier, location, tags, urgency, status, pinning, duplication and local expiry.

Why it exists

Ward jobs often end up scattered. This keeps the temporary list quicker to capture, easier to scan and less likely to drift during a shift.

Current status

Prototype, field-tested personally and narrow on purpose. Useful enough to keep using; not production software and not a clinical system.

Current prototype

Fast local capture for ward jobs

  • Start with free text, then add structured fields when useful.
  • Use radial menus for common note and location shortcuts.
  • Keep the first favourites wheel-sized for one-handed use.
  • Set status, urgency, job type, pins, bump-to-top and duplicate-job actions.
  • Compact card mode and local auto-expiry for dense active lists.
  • No backend, login, cloud sync, analytics, messaging, AI or EHR integration.

Clinical safety boundary

Not a medical record. Not decision support.

This is a personal prototype for temporary task organisation only. It is not a referral app, EHR, messaging system, NHS-approved product, clinical safety system, regulated medical device or GDPR-assured service. It should not be used as the source of truth for patient care.

Runtime data should stay on the device. Do not send patient-identifiable data to this website or repository.
